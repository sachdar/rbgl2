Frequently Asked Questions

Below you will find answers to the most common questions about Rind Baloch Global Logistics and our operations as an International NVOCC. For further details, please visit our official website: www.rindbaloch.com.

A Non-Vessel Operating Common Carrier (NVOCC) is a logistics provider that issues its own bills of lading and arranges ocean freight shipments without owning vessels. Rind Baloch Global Logistics operates as an International NVOCC, partnering with leading carriers such as MSC, COSCO, CMA CGM, ONE Line, Yang Ming, HMM, and TS Lines to provide seamless transport solutions.

RBGL provides a comprehensive range of logistics services including: • Ocean Freight (FCL/LCL) • Trucking and inland transport • Dangerous Goods (DG) cargo handling • Out-of-Gauge (OOG) cargo solutions • ISO tank services • Container trading and leasing • Customs clearance and cargo consolidation Full details are available on our official website: www.rindbaloch.com.

Customers can track shipments directly through our website rindbaloch.com using their booking reference or container number. Our tracking system provides real-time updates on cargo status and location.

RBGL is headquartered in Karachi, Pakistan, with operations extending across major international trade routes. We serve clients globally, coordinating shipments to and from Asia, Europe, the Middle East, and North America.
