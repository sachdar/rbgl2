Privacy Policy

At Rind Baloch Global Logistics, we respect your privacy and are committed to protecting your personal information. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and safeguard your data when you interact with our services.

Information We Collect

We may collect personal details such as your name, company, contact information, shipment details, billing information, and communications when you engage our services or contact us.

How We Use Information

We use your information to provide logistics services, process shipments, manage customer accounts, improve support, comply with legal obligations, and communicate important updates related to your shipments and services.

Legal Basis

We process personal data based on contractual necessity, legitimate interests in operating our business, and compliance with applicable international regulations.

Data Protection

We implement industry-standard technical and organizational measures to protect your data against unauthorized access, alteration, disclosure, or destruction. Access is restricted to authorized personnel and trusted partners.

Sharing of Information

We may share data with carriers, agents, customs and regulatory authorities, and service providers strictly for service fulfillment, compliance, and fraud prevention. We do not sell personal data.

Data Retention

We retain personal data only as long as necessary to fulfill the purposes outlined in this policy, meet legal requirements, and resolve disputes. Retention periods vary by record type and regulatory obligations.

Your Rights

Subject to applicable law, you may request access, correction, deletion, restriction, or portability of your data. To exercise these rights, please contact us using the details below.

International Transfers

As an International NVOCC, your data may be transferred across borders to facilitate logistics operations. We take appropriate safeguards to protect data during such transfers.

Cookies & Tracking

Our website may use cookies and similar technologies to improve functionality and analyze usage. You can manage cookie preferences through your browser settings.

Policy Updates

We may update this Privacy Policy periodically. The latest version will always be available on this page, with the effective date indicated below.
