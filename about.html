Who We Are

Rind Baloch Global Logistics is a professionally managed Non‑Vessel Operating Common Carrier (NVOCC) and international freight forwarding company. We are dedicated to providing reliable, efficient, and cost‑effective global logistics solutions.

Our Expertise

Established with the vision to deliver premium logistics solutions, RBGL has grown into a trusted partner for clients across the globe. With years of operational experience in ocean freight, trucking, handling of dangerous goods cargos, OOG equipment, ISO tanks, cargo consolidation, documentation, custom clearance, TIR convention, and container trading, we bring unmatched expertise to every shipment.
